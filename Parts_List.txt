# Quadruped Robot Project – Parts List (BOM)
# Repository-ready text file
# Maintained by: Krish
# Last updated: 2026-01-15

====================================================
1. COMPUTE & CONTROL
====================================================
- NVIDIA Jetson Orin Nano
  Role: High-level control, perception, future autonomy
  OS: Ubuntu (JetPack)

- ESP32 Development Board
  Role: Low-level servo control, gait timing, real-time tasks
  Interface: UART / I2C with Jetson

- PCA9685 16-Channel PWM Servo Driver
  Role: Generates stable PWM signals for servos
  Interface: I2C
  Voltage: 5–6 V logic-compatible

====================================================
2. ACTUATION
====================================================
- DS3240MG High Torque Servo Motors (270°)
  Quantity: 12 (3 per leg × 4 legs)
  Positions:
    - Shoulder (Hip)
    - Mid-limb (Thigh/Knee equivalent)
    - Foot (Ankle)
  Notes:
    - No position feedback
    - Metal gears
    - High stall torque suitable for load-bearing

====================================================
3. POWER SYSTEM
====================================================
- 4S LiPo Battery – 6200 mAh
  Nominal Voltage: 14.8 V
  Fully Charged: 16.8 V
  Role: Main power source

- DC Buck Converter (15–20 V → 5 V)
  Role: Step-down voltage for servos and logic
  Features:
    - Current protection
    - High current capability

- Power Distribution Wiring
  Wire Gauges Used:
    - 10 AWG (main battery lines)
    - 12 AWG (high-current servo rails)
    - 18–22 AWG (logic and signal)

- XT60 / XT90 Connectors
  Role: Battery connection and power safety

====================================================
4. SENSORS
====================================================
- MPU6050 IMU
  Sensors:
    - 3-axis Accelerometer
    - 3-axis Gyroscope
  Role:
    - Orientation estimation
    - Stability feedback
    - PD control reference

====================================================
5. MECHANICAL STRUCTURE
====================================================
- 3D Printed Parts (Material: PETG)
  Components:
    - Main torso frame
    - Shoulder brackets
    - Mid-limb links
    - Foot links
    - Servo mounts
    - Cable routing guides

- Fasteners
  Types:
    - M2 screws and nuts
    - M3 screws and nuts
  Usage:
    - Servo mounting
    - Structural joints

- Bearings / Bushings (where applicable)
  Role: Reduce friction at rotating joints

====================================================
6. FEET & GROUND INTERFACE
====================================================
- Rubber Feet / Pads
  Role:
    - Shock absorption
    - Improved grip
    - Noise reduction

- Optional TPU foot caps
  Role:
    - Compliance
    - Vibration damping

====================================================
7. COMMUNICATION & INTERFACES
====================================================
- I2C Wiring
  Used for:
    - PCA9685
    - MPU6050

- UART Wiring
  Used for:
    - Jetson ↔ ESP32 communication

- USB Cables
  Role:
    - Programming
    - Debugging

====================================================
8. TOOLS & SUPPORTING HARDWARE
====================================================
- Soldering Iron & Solder Wire
- Heat Shrink Tubes
- Multimeter
- Zip Ties / Cable Sleeves
- Thread Locker (medium strength)

====================================================
9. SOFTWARE STACK (REFERENCE)
====================================================
- Ubuntu (Jetson)
- Python
- NumPy
- Matplotlib (visualization & kinematics)
- Custom PD Controllers
- FSM-based gait & limb control
- I2C & GPIO libraries

====================================================
10. NOTES
====================================================
- Architecture is stability-first and reflex-based
- Designed for incremental upgrades:
  - Encoders
  - Force sensors
  - Vision modules
  - ROS 2 integration
